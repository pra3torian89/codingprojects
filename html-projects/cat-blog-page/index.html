About

Hello there! I am Andrew, a lifelong animal lover and aspiring full-stack developer with a passion for storytelling and creative writing. This blog is a cozy corner of the internet where I get to share a little joy, one whisker at a time.

Let me introduce you to Roxy—my sweet, cuddly Ragdoll kitty who stole our hearts from day one. With her soft fur, gentle personality, and love for lap time, Roxy was more than a pet—she was family. This blog is a tribute to the warmth and wonder she brought into our lives, and a place for fellow cat enthusiasts to smile, relate, and maybe even shed a happy tear.

Roxy and Her Cozy Hoodie

One of the sweetest things about Roxy was how much she loved her little hoodie—especially after getting her lion cut. With her long, fluffy fur trimmed down, she'd get a little chilly on cooler days, and the moment we slipped her hoddie on, she'd melt right into it like it was the coziest thing in the world.

She looked absolutely adorable—her soft blue eyes peeking out from the hood, her tiny paws tucked in like a fashion model. It wasn't just for warmth either; she seemed to know how cute she looked and would strut around the house with this slow, regal confidcence. Our little queen, wrapped in fleece.
