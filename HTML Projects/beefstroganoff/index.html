Beef Stroganoff

This modern beef stroganoff recipe is on of my family's all-time favorites! It is simple to make and will have you eating hearty for several days.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. egg noodles
  • 1 lb. lean ground beef
  • 1 medium to large yellow onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 family-size can of condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 8 oz. sour cream
  • 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp herb de provence
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, melt the butter, then add onions and dried herbs once butter is fully melted.
  2. Cook onions until caramelized. This should take 45 minutes to 1 hour. Once caramelized, add the garlic.
  3. Once the garlic is fragrent, add the ground been and cook until brown, straining off any excess grease.
  4. When the ground beef is fully-cooked, add in the cream of mushroom soup, sour cream, and worcestershire sauce. Stir to combine. Cook until mixture comes to a simmer. Add salt and fresh-craked black pepper to taste.
  5. In a separate pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil and cook the egg noodles.
  6. When the noodles are al dente, remove from heat and strain.
  7. Serve the stroganoff over the egg noodles. Alternatively, you may serve over white rice.
